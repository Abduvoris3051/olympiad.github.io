About us

EnterOlymp is an innovative online platform catering to high school students aiming to excel in international and Russian subject olympiads. A comprehensive, one-stop hub for information and resources tailored to enhance success in diverse olympiads.

Creating EnterOlymp stemmed from a collective recognition of significant challenges faced by remote school students. In essence, EnterOlymp was born out of the shared vision to open access to academic excellence and empower students worldwide with the resources they need.
